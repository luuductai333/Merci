Merci brand production team

Cao Van Minh – CEO & Founder

As the soul and leader of Merci, Cao Van Minh is not only a talented founder but also a leader with strategic vision. With many years of experience in the fashion industry, Minh has given Merci a clear and sustainable direction. He always strives to find new trends, combining art and commerce to create unique business strategies. Under Minh's leadership, Merci not only developed strongly but also affirmed its position in the hearts of customers.

Tran Dieu Linh – Creative Director

Tran Dieu Linh is the inspiration and creative heart of Merci. With a strong passion for fashion and art, Linh has brought to Merci unique, sophisticated and stylish fashion collections. She constantly explores and creates, bringing designs that are not only beautiful but also clearly reflect the personality and spirit of modern women. Linh always strives to make each Merci product a work of art, bringing spiritual value and confidence to the wearer.

Truong Hoai Thuong – Marketing Director

With extensive experience in the field of marketing and communications, Truong Hoai Thuong has helped Merci build and develop a strong brand image. Thuong is the person behind creative and effective marketing strategies, helping Merci reach and conquer more customers. She is always looking for new ways to promote the brand, build close relationships with customers and enhance the value of the Merci brand in the fashion market.

Phi Quoc Trieu – Chief Financial Officer

Phi Quoc Trieu is responsible for financial management and ensuring financial stability for Merci. With solid knowledge and long experience in the financial sector, Trieu always ensures that every business decision is supported by detailed and effective financial plans. He plays an important role in maintaining Merci's balance and sustainable development, helping the brand not only grow strongly but also be financially sustainable.

Nguyen The Manh – Production Director

Nguyen The Manh is the person who supervises the entire production process and ensures product quality of Merci. With rich experience in the manufacturing industry and supply chain management, Manh ensures that each Merci product meets the highest standards of quality and is produced sustainably. He is always looking for new ways to improve production processes, ensure efficiency and minimize environmental impact. Manh not only ensures perfection in each product but also contributes to Merci's sustainable development.
